## Current research

### Spatial omics & chemotherapy response (MSc TAPM)

**Title:** Integrating Spatial Omics, Signal Transduction Pathway Analysis, and Machine Learning to Predict Chemotherapy Response in Colorectal Cancer [file:1]

Colorectal cancer has high global incidence and mortality, yet fewer than 20% of stage III patients respond to 5‑FU–based chemotherapy. [file:1] This project integrates bulk transcriptomics, spatial single‑cell proteomics, and clinical covariates to predict chemotherapy response and identify novel biomarkers rooted in the tumour microenvironment. [file:1]

In this repo I focus on:
- RNA‑seq pre‑processing, QC, and pathway‑level signatures (e.g. proliferation, apoptosis, EMT). [file:1]  
- Spatial feature engineering from stained tissue sections (cell identity, state, and neighbourhoods). [file:1]  
- Supervised / deep learning models for multimodal prediction and model interpretability. [file:1]
